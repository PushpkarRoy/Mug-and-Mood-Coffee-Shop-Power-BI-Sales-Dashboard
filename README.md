## 📌 Project Overview

The **Mug & Mood Coffee Shop** Sales Dashboard is a real-world **Power BI project** designed to analyze and visualize sales performance data of a fictional but realistic Indian coffee shop chain.

This project helps stakeholders and business owners:
- Monitor monthly and category-wise performance
- Identify top-performing products and cities
- Track KPIs like revenue, order count, average price, and customer behavior

> 📍 Location: Indian cities only  
> 🧠 Domain: Retail / Food & Beverage  
> 🎯 Goal: Data-driven decisions for better sales and marketing strategy

## 📊 Dashboard Features

✅ **KPIs Tracked:**
- 📦 Total Sales
- 🧾 Total Orders
- 👥 Unique Customers
- 💸 Average Order Value

✅ **Visual Insights:**
- 📅 Monthly sales trend
- 🏙️ City-wise sales performance
- 🍰 Category-wise product sales
- 🔝 Top 5 products by revenue
- 📈 Profit trend by product line

✅ **Filters & Slicers:**
- Month
- Product Category
- City
- Customer Age Group
